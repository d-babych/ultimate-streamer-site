Ultimate Frisbee Live Streaming from iPhone

Ultimate frisbee is fast and full of quick momentum changes. A good live stream needs more than just a camera: viewers need to see the score, follow the game situation and understand what is happening even if they join in the middle of a point.

Ultimate Streamer helps clubs, federations and tournament organizers broadcast ultimate games with just an iPhone. The app includes a scoreboard, timer, replays engine, sponsor videos and built-in YouTube streaming.

Why ultimate frisbee needs a sports-first streaming app

Many teams, especially youth and amateur teams, already stream their games from a phone. But plain video is not enough. People in the comments ask about the score and the time. They want to feel more involved.

Ultimate Streamer is built specifically for sports broadcasts. So the essential game information is visible directly on screen: team names, score, timer, periods, possession and branding. All without bringing a laptop, capture card or full production crew.
