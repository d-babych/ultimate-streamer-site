Basketball Live Streaming from iPhone

Basketball is fast, emotional and full of momentum changes. A good live stream needs more than just a camera: viewers need to see the score, follow the period time and understand the game situation even if they join in the middle of a quarter.

Ultimate Streamer helps basketball clubs, schools and tournament organizers broadcast games with just an iPhone. The app includes a scoreboard, timer, replays engine, sponsor videos and built-in YouTube streaming.

Why basketball needs a sports-first streaming app

Many youth and amateur basketball clubs already record their games. But plain video is often not enough. Parents and fans want to follow the score, understand the period time and watch the game live instead of waiting for a recording.

Ultimate Streamer is built specifically for sports broadcasts. So the essential game information is visible directly on screen: team names, score, timer, periods, replays and branding. All without bringing a laptop, capture card or full production crew.
