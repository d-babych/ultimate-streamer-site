Flag Football Live Streaming from iPhone

Flag football is fast, tactical and full of explosive moments. A good live stream needs more than just a camera: viewers need to see the score, follow the game situation and understand what is happening during every drive.

Ultimate Streamer helps flag football teams, clubs and tournament organizers broadcast games with just an iPhone. The app includes a scoreboard, timer, replays engine, sponsor videos and built-in YouTube streaming.

Why flag football needs a sports-first streaming app

Many teams already publish videos after the game. But live streaming creates stronger engagement: players, families and fans can follow the match in real time and share key moments online.

Ultimate Streamer is built specifically for sports broadcasts. So the essential game information is visible directly on screen: team names, score, timer, replays and branding. All without bringing a laptop, capture card or full production crew.
